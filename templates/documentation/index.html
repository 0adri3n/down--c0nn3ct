What is down-c0nn3ct ?

down-c0nn3ct is an online MarkDown editor with an integrated-preview, lightweight and newbie-friendly.

What is MarkDown ?

For the newbies, a little definition :

Markdown is a lightweight markup language that you can use to add formatting elements to plaintext text documents.

Why MarkDown ?

Because it's fun and easy-to-use ! You don't need any programming knowledge to use it, and I promise you can learn it in 10 minutes.

It's one the best way to create documents to learn or take notes at school or at work.

H4sh Tech™

What is this ? It's a storage system that I thinked about to avoid the creation of account.

Get to know it !

Basically, H4sh Tech™ just identify your file with a fully random hash when added to the database.

Thanks to this, each file are fully-private and non-accessible by others

Use it

You just save your file and note the returned hash ! Saving your hash can be done by noting it down on your Discord server, a note, a WhatsApp message and many other way.

The benefits of H4sh Tech™ is that you can even share your file to your friends thanks to the hash ! Certainly, if your friend modifies your original file, you won't see it but he can share you a new hash.

I recognize that this system is clearly storage-expensive and is not economic at all, but the database will be cleared every 2 months I think. Trying to balance this system a bit, I compress the file's content in the database.
